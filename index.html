About

Wilson Fanestil is a multidisciplinary artist, graphic designer, web developer and audio engineer. Wilson works freelance for a variety of concientious clients including clothing brands, record labels, start-ups and open source initiatives. Wilson grew up in Boulder Colorado, recieved his B.F.A. in Recorded Music from the Clive Davis Inst. at Tisch School of the Arts in New York and has lived and worked in Spain, Germany and the United States.
